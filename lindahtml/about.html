The Boss

Linda’s is a family-owned business in California, originally a small tortillería run by a Mexican immigrant family. Now, the second-generation owner, Linda, has taken over and wants to turn it into a modern Tex-Mex diner that appeals to the new, gentrified neighborhood while staying true to its roots. she's renamed the business and wants to maintain the traditions of her family.
